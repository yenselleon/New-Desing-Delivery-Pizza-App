# Product Definition - Pizza Delivery Dashboard

## Initial Concept
This project is a modern, web-based platform designed for customers to browse, customize, and order pizzas. It focuses on providing a seamless, app-like experience with high-quality visuals and an intuitive interface.

## Target Audience
- **Primary Users:** Customers looking for a convenient way to order pizza online.
- **User Needs:** A fast, reliable, and visually appealing way to explore the menu and place orders from any device.

## Core Goals
- **Intuitive Ordering:** Provide an effortless flow for browsing the menu and placing orders.
- **Customization:** Enable customers to tailor their pizzas exactly to their liking through an interactive builder.
- **High Performance:** Ensure a smooth, "app-like" user experience with fast transitions and responsive design.

## Key Features
- **Interactive Pizza Builder:** A core feature allowing users to select dough types, sauces, and toppings with real-time visual feedback.
- **Visual Menu:** A rich, image-focused menu showcasing high-quality photos of pizzas and ingredients to drive engagement.
- **Streamlined Checkout:** A clean and efficient process for finalizing orders.

## Visual Identity & UX
- **App-like Experience:** Focused on smooth interactions and a mobile-first philosophy.
- **Modern & Clean:** A contemporary interface that prioritizes ease of use and reduces friction.
- **Imagery-Driven:** Heavy emphasis on high-quality visuals to enhance the ordering experience.
